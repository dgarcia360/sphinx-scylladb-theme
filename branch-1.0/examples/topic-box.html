Default Topic¶

With Icon¶

Using:

.. topic-box::
    :title: Lorem Ipsum
    :icon: fa fa-github
    :link: #
    :anchor: Lorem ipsum

    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit.

Results in:

Lorem Ipsum
